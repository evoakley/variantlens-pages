VariantLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

VariantLens is a Shopify app that helps merchants assign product images to individual product variants. This privacy policy explains what information VariantLens collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Access

VariantLens accesses Shopify store information needed to provide the app, including product data, variant data, product images, and image assignment settings created by the merchant.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to operate VariantLens, save image assignments, display the correct images for selected variants, provide support, and maintain app security and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell merchant data. We do not share merchant data with third parties except where required to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, or provide support.

Data Storage

Variant image assignment data is stored securely and used only for app functionality. If a merchant uninstalls VariantLens, app-related data may be deleted in accordance with Shopify requirements and our internal retention practices.
